Best Chocotorta in the world

One chocotorta recipe that'll have you licking your fingers, pa.

Ingredients

  • 400 g of dulce de leche
  • 400 g of heavy cream or custard
  • 750 g of chocolate cookies
  • Milk or coffee for dipping the cookies
  • 50 g of semi-sweet chocolate
  • 50 g of cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Start beating the dulce de leche, and when it becomes a lighter color, add the cream. Beat on the lowest mixer setting until it reaches a "ribbon stage." Be very careful not to overbeat, or the mixture will curdle!

  2. Step 2

    If by some misfortune it does curdle, add a splash of cream and gently mix it in. Set the mixture aside.

  3. Step 3

    Dip each cookie in milk or coffee and arrange them in a dish to form a layer. Add a layer of the dulce de leche cream mixture. Then add another layer of moistened cookies, and repeat this process 4 times.

  4. Step 4

    Refrigerate for 1 hour.

  5. Step 5

    Dust with cocoa powder. When serving, grate a bit of dark chocolate on top, and enjoy your chocotorta!
